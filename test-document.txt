COMPANY HANDBOOK - SAMPLE CONTENT

WORK SCHEDULE POLICY:
Standard work hours are 9 AM to 5 PM, with flexible start times between 8 AM and 10 AM. 
Core collaboration hours are 10 AM to 4 PM.

REMOTE WORK POLICY:
Employees can work remotely up to 3 days per week. 
Remote work requests must be approved by managers.

TIME OFF POLICY:
- 20 PTO days per year
- 10 company holidays
- Unlimited sick leave with manager approval

BENEFITS:
- Health, dental, and vision insurance
- 401(k) with 4% company match
- Stock options after 1 year of employment

DRESS CODE:
Business casual. No formal dress code requirements.

MEETING POLICY:
No meetings on Fridays. All meetings should have clear agendas and end on time.
